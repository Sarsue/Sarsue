About Me

The Journey So Far

My career kicked off at Nortac Defence Canada Ltd., where I worked on a team that developed and tested embedded software and middleware for Echo, an IP68-rated satellite tracking device. This project ensured secure, real-time communication, GPS tracking, and compliance with defence standards across multiple platforms. My contributions also led to a U.S. patent for situational awareness over low-bandwidth satellite systems.

In 2019, I joined Contextere as a Lead Engineer, where I led the end-to-end development of Madison, an AI-powered enterprise application integrated with Microsoft Teams. Combining React, C#, Python, and Azure AI models, I delivered secure, scalable, and high-performance solutions for B2B and government clients.

As a Microsoft Certified Azure Solutions Architect Expert, I architect cloud solutions that are secure, scalable, and robust. I’ve also deployed apps across AWS, Google Cloud, and DigitalOcean, optimizing performance and cost-efficiency.

What I’m Building Now

My latest project, SynTextAI, leverages AI to understand documents, extracting insights that drive smarter decisions and unlock untapped potential.
