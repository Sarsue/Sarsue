About Me

Personal Journey

My fascination with technology began in 1997 when a bulky personal computer arrived at our home. What started as a curiosity about spreadsheets and databases quickly became a deep passion for problem-solving. By the time I encountered Pascal programming in high school, I realized that coding wasn’t just a skill—it was a way of thinking.

University cemented this mindset. Initially set on Mechanical/ Aerospace Engineering, I pivoted to Computer Systems Engineering, drawn by the thrill of building real-world solutions. From my first exposure to embedded systems to developing an exam scheduling platform for my final-year project, I saw firsthand how technology could transform ideas into impact.

Professional Experience

I started my career at a situational command and control (SCC) startup, where I developed mission-critical software. One of my most rewarding projects was Echo, a satellite device enabling secure beyond-line-of-sight (BLOS) communication for special forces. This work, which integrated with the Track24 SCC TITAN system, earned a U.S. patent—proof that technology, when applied right, can save lives.

Another defining moment came when I ported our AES encryption module to LUA, securing a high-stakes Saudi contract. This project reinforced my ability to bridge technical challenges with strategic business needs.

In 2019, I transitioned to Contextere, leading the development of an AI-driven platform for blue-collar workers. My focus was ensuring that AI wasn’t just cutting-edge but also practical—delivering real value where it mattered most.

As a Microsoft Certified: Azure Solutions Architect Expert, I design and implement scalable, secure, and resilient cloud solutions. Beyond Azure, I have hands-on experience deploying applications on AWS, Google Cloud, and DigitalOcean, leveraging cloud-native technologies to optimize performance and cost.

Present Focus

Today, I specialize in building scalable AI solutions that solve real business challenges. My latest venture, SynTextAI, is designed to extract meaningful insights from documents using AI, unlocking hidden potential in information and enabling smarter decision-making.
